Hello World! I am Luyan. I am currently a post-doc fellow in Department of Psychology, University of Hong Kong, working with Professor William Hayward. Before moving to Hong Kong in 2018, I studied in cap-lab, Ghent University in Belgium for PhD, under the supervision of Professor Gilles Pourtois. My disseration was about Multiple face processing. I received Master of Sciences in Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (supervisor: Professor Wenfeng Chen) in 2014, and Bachelor of Sciences in Beijing Forestry University in 2011.

I am broadly interested in understanding human behavior using a variety of methods, and passionate about solving both basic and applied problems in user researcher. The methods I mainly used are:

  • psychophysics
  • EEG
  • eye traker
  • computational modelling
  • surveys
